
(i) Standard: Uses and disclosures consistent 
with notice. A covered entity that is required 
by §164.520 to have a notice may not use or 
disclose protected health information in a manner
 inconsistent with such notice. A covered entity
 that is required by §164.520(b)(1)(iii) to 
include a specific statement in its notice if it 
intends to engage in an activity listed in 
§164.520(b)(1)(iii)(A)–(C), may not use or 
disclose protected health information for such 
activities, unless the required statement is 
included in the notice.
